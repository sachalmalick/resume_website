Mpath

Mpath is a platform built to facilitate cashless payments for homeless persons in the Lower East Side.

Homeless persons can request an account from a local administrator, who will generate a QR code that they will print on a poster board

Generous pedestrians can scan the QR code to be taken to a site where they can see a biography and picture of the homeless person, and they can make a donation through paypal.

After any period of time, the homeless person can visit.
